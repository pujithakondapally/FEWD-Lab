Audio Model

Teachable Machine: Training an Audio Model to Distinguish Koyal Bird and Rooster Sounds

Using the Teachable Machine audio model, I embarked on training my model with distinct bird sounds, specifically the melodious calls of the Koyal Bird and the crowing of the Rooster. By feeding the model these unique audio samples, it learned to differentiate and identify the two bird species based on their distinct sound patterns. The Teachable Machine's versatility allowed me to create an accurate and personalized classification system for these avian sounds, opening up possibilities for various applications in birdwatching, conservation, and soundscape analysis.

Leveraging the Teachable Machine's pose model, I trained my model using two contrasting yoga poses: the graceful Crescent Lunge Pose and the stability-focused Bridge Pose.
